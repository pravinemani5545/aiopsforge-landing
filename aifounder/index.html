how it works

Plug your tools in once, pick your workflows from the library, let the AI hub run them on schedule. You approve outputs before they ship — every time.

questions

Everything else you'd ask.

How is this different from Zapier or Make?
Zapier is plumbing — it connects tools but doesn't decide anything. You still write the logic. aifounder.ca ships pre-built workflows with AI logic baked in — the morning brief writes itself, the follow-ups personalize themselves, the metrics report drafts itself. You configure the goal in plain English; the workflow handles the steps.
How is this different from hiring an EA or VA?
An EA caps at ~40 hours/week, takes 2-4 weeks to ramp, costs $3,000-8,000/mo, has off-days. aifounder.ca runs 24/7, ramps in 30 minutes, costs $49-199/mo, never has off-days. The trade-off: an EA can attend a Zoom call. aifounder.ca can't. Use aifounder for everything else, hire human help for the parts that need a human voice.
Do I need to be technical?
No. Connect tools with one-click OAuth. Configure workflows with a natural-language brief ("send a renewal nudge to anyone whose pilot is >7 days from expiry"). No code, no field-by-field forms.
Can I trust AI with my business?
Nothing ships without your sign-off. Every draft, follow-up, and report lands in your inbox or Slack with [✓ send] [edit] [kill] buttons. You stay in the loop on every output. The AI saves you the 90% drafting time, you keep the 10% review + approval.
What if my business is unique — these workflows don't fit?
The 14 default workflows cover 80% of solo-operator needs. For the other 20%, the Operator plan includes 2 custom workflow builds per quarter — you describe what you need, we build it, you own it. AOF community members also share + remix custom workflows in the community library.
How does the AIOpsForge bundle work?
If you're an AIOpsForge member ($9.99/mo), you automatically get the Founder plan free — full $99/mo value bundled. Connect on day 1, all 14 workflows unlocked. Cancel AOF and you keep your data + workflows for 60 days before they freeze.
What happens to my data?
Read-only OAuth — we don't store credentials. Workflow outputs are encrypted at rest, evicted from cache within 24 hours of approval, and never used to train models. You can export your full ledger + reports as JSON anytime.
