questions

Everything else you'd ask.

How is this different from buying API access directly?
Two things: one balance for every tool, and no per-tool subscription. With direct APIs you'd manage 8+ accounts, 8+ invoices, 8+ rate limits, and switch costs every time a better model ships. Here, you top up once and any tool draws from the same balance — including image, video, voice, research, code, and agent runs.
Do credits expire?
No. Buy 5,000 credits today, use them in three years. We don't burn down your balance with renewal cycles.
How does the AIOpsForge bundle work?
If you're an AIOpsForge member ($9.99/mo), you automatically get 2,000 credits per month on aifounder.ca — refilled on your billing cycle. Use them on any tool. Unused credits roll over indefinitely. Cancel AOF and your bundled credits stop, but everything you've already accumulated stays.
What's the unit cost per task?
Every tool shows live unit cost before you run. Rough averages: image generation $0.04–$0.40 depending on model, video $0.30–$2 per second, voice synth ~$0.05 per minute, deep research $0.02–$0.15 per query, code assist ~$0.01 per call. Credits = $0.01 each (volume discount drops it lower).
Can my team share one balance?
Yes — on the Operator pack and above. Unlimited seats on one shared balance. Per-seat usage tracking, per-project ledger, monthly statements broken down by member. No per-seat charge.
Which models do you support?
As of 2026 Q2: Claude (Anthropic), GPT-5 (OpenAI), Gemini 3 (Google), Kimi K2 (Moonshot), Nano Banana Pro + Flux (image), VEO 3.1 + Sora 2 + Runway (video), ElevenLabs + Suno + Hedra (audio), Perplexity (research). We add models within 2 weeks of public release. Models retire when usage drops below 1% — you're auto-migrated to the closest equivalent.
What happens to my data?
We don't store your prompts or outputs by default — they pass through to the underlying provider, then evict from our edge cache within 24 hours. Enable project archival in settings if you want a persistent ledger of generations. We never train on your data.
