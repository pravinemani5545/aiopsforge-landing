questions

Frequently Asked Questions
Why SMS and not a regular app?
Because you already check your texts 100+ times a day. You don't need another app icon you'll ignore after a week. SMS has zero friction — no login, no loading screen, no syncing. You text a task and it's done. The agent meets you where you already are.
Does it work with iMessage / Android / WhatsApp?
It works via standard SMS to any phone number. iMessage, Android Messages, any carrier. WhatsApp and Telegram support are on the roadmap for v2. You'll get a dedicated number to text — save it as a contact and it feels native.
How does it remember everything?
The agent uses persistent memory backed by a database. Every task, every conversation, every project — stored and indexed. When you ask "what did I do last week?" it pulls from your full history. No context resets between sessions.
What if I accidentally text it something personal?
The agent only processes task-related content. It doesn't read your other texts — it only sees messages sent to its dedicated number. Your data is stored securely and never shared. You can delete your entire history anytime with one text: "delete everything."
Is there a limit on how many tasks or texts I can send?
Unlimited tasks, unlimited texts. The flat monthly price covers everything. No per-message fees, no token limits you'll hit. Use it as much as you need to.
