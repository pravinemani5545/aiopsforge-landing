questions

The ones I actually get.

If yours isn't here, ask on the call. Every objection below is a real one I've heard.

Why don't you list prices on this page?

Because pricing depends on your stack, the data I see on the call, and how custom the scope ends up being. I'd rather tell you a real number in writing within 24 hours after we talk than post a sticker price that doesn't fit your situation. The 30-min call is free either way.

What stack do you build on?

Claude Code + Vercel (serverless + cron + KV) + Anthropic API. I work in your stack if it matches — TypeScript, Next.js, Supabase, Stripe, Beehiiv, Whop are all comfortable. I won't build on n8n, Make, or Zapier — fragile, expensive at scale, and you can't read the code six months from now.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes — happy to. Standard mutual NDA at the scoping-call stage. For audits where I'm interviewing your team, I sign before any conversation happens.

Who owns the code?

You do, outright. All deliverables are work-for-hire under a written agreement. You get full source, the deployment, and the right to modify or sell. I keep no claim.

Why fixed price instead of hourly?

Hourly aligns incentives badly — I'd be incentivized to take longer, you'd be incentivized to micro-manage. Fixed-price aligns us on outcome: we both want the thing done well, fast, and right the first time.

What if I'm in a different timezone?

I'm in Toronto (ET). Async-default works for most engagements — Slack/email + recorded Loom updates. Live calls in your timezone within 9am–9pm ET; outside that, written-first.

Do you do equity-for-work?

No. Cash, fair price, no equity entanglement.

What if the scope changes mid-project?

For sprints: scope is fixed, period. For audits + custom builds: anything outside the written spec is a separate quote. I'll tell you the impact before you decide, never silently re-scope.

Can you start next week?

Usually yes for sprints, sometimes for custom builds. Audits typically book 2–3 weeks out. Always ask — calendars change.

What if I'm not sure I need an AI engagement at all?

Book the call anyway. If I don't think AI is the right fix, I'll tell you that on the call and point you to who or what is. The 30 minutes are free; the honest answer is the deliverable.
