faq

Common questions

What happens if I run out of credits?
You can purchase additional credit packs or wait for your monthly reset. Free tier resets on the 1st.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Yes. Changes take effect immediately. No lock-in, no annual commitment.
What counts as one "run"?
One execution of a tool from start to finish. Multi-step tools (like the onboarding agent) count as a single run.
Is there a refund policy?
Cancel anytime with one click through Whop. No questions asked.
